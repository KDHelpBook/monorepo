# Tips

A few things that make the reader nicer to use.

- Press a keyword in the **Index** to jump straight to a topic — synonyms help
  readers find things the way *they* would search.
- **Favorites** remember the pages you return to.
- The **Search** tab does an offline full-text search across every loaded docset.
- Load more than one docset and they merge into a single table of contents,
  index, and search — this page comes from a *second* book.
