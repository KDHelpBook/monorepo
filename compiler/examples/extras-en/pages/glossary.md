# Glossary

**Docset** — a self-contained `.khb` file: one book of documentation plus its
prebuilt table of contents, index and search.

**Collection** — several docsets loaded together and merged into one navigation.

**Facet** — a category tag that cuts across the table-of-contents tree.

**Keyword** — an index term (F1-style) that points at one or more topics.
