kdhelp — quick reference card
=============================

Compile a docset:
  kdhelp compile <src-dir> -o out.khb
  kdhelp compile <src-dir> -o out.khb --assets sidecar   (attachments -> out.khba)

Convert:
  kdhelp convert out.khb  -o out.khbb
  kdhelp convert out.khbb -o out.khb

Publish:
  kdhelp pack  --viewer viewer-ts/dist --docset out.khb -o publish/
  kdhelp patch publish/ --docset new.khb

Assets:
  Put files under assets/ and link them as assets/<name>.
  Images render inline; other files become download links.
  A .khb may be backed by several sidecar .khba packs.
